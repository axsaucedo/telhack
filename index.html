TELHack is Health Education England's premier hackathon aimed at transforming healthcare education

TELHack will bring together 80 of the UK’s best developers to create new technologies for healthcare education positioning the UK as the leading country for innovation in healthcare education. A Hack Partners event which is being run under the NHS Health Education England (HEE) Technology Enhanced Learning (TEL) programme.

TELHack will take place in a unique immersive venue in London in November 2015. The hackathon will bring together technology giants and public bodies such as the NHS and HEE to innovate in healthcare education.

The world's brightest minds, building high-impact solutions for the industry.

Innovating healthcare education

TELHack is Health Education England's flagship hackathon aimed at innovating healthcare education. A leading event bringing together 80 of the best developers, designers and industry experts from all around the UK to conceive, build and launch high impact solutions that will revolutionise healthcare education.


At unique, immersive spaces

Located in the heart of London, the TELHack hackathon will take place in unique immersive spaces. The winning team will have a chance to present their winning projects to the industry at the National Science Museum. This will be the perfect venue, offering a unique, immersive experience, allowing participants to be part of the industry they will be hacking throughout the weekend. This will be the perfect environment to create the next-generation revolutionary Startups.


Cutting edge gadgets, APIs, Datasets

We will be providing a set of gadgets, access to APIs and datasets relevant to the healthcare education sector that will help participants in their startup journeys. Throughout the whole weekend, there will be access to 3D printers, Google Glass, Oculus Rift, Leap Motion, Myo Devices, and other kit and equipment. There will be access to several APIs, and selected datasets will be made available.


Unbelievable speakers, judges and mentors

The hackathon will be kick-started with talks from senior executives from leading healthcare companies and corporations. Mentors will be technology and industry professionals and the judging panel will include venture capitalists, investors and industry experts. TELHack aims to provide the right environment to ensure all attendees have access to expert advice and judgement throughout the whole event, in order for them to truly build solutions that will revolutionise healthcare education as we know it.


Let's make this happen

The hackathon is organised by Hack Partners - the same organisation that ran the HackTrain, StartupBus UK 2015, StartupHacks, HackaGlobal and many other successful hackathons. TELHack will be the largest hackathon aimed at innovating healthcare education.
