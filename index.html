Health Education England's signature hackathon targeted to revolutionise Health Education.

TELHack is the UK’s largest initiative to innovate the health professions’ education arena and position the UK as the number one country for innovation in healthcare education in the world. TELHack is a Hack Partners event which is being run under the NHS Health Education England (HEE) Technology Enhanced Learning (TEL) programme.

The TELHack 2015 will take place in a unique venue, relevant to the initiative itself ­in London. This initiative will bring together the world’s brightest technologists, the Silicon Valley tech giants, the largest healthcare education players around the UK, and public bodies such as the NHS and HEE on a mission to revolutionise health education.

Innovating Health Education

TELHack is Health Education England's flagship hackathon aimed to innovate the retail industry. A high impact hackathon, where 80 of the best developers, designers and industry experts from all around Europe will conceive, build and launch solutions that will revolutionize Health Education. During this hackathon participants will propose high-impact soluations to innovate this sector.


Cutting edge gadgets, APIs, Datasets

We will be providing a set of gadgets, access to APIs and Datasets relevant to the retail industry that will help participants in their Startup journeys. Throughout the whole weekend, we will provide access to 3D printers, Google Glass, Oculus Rift, Leap Motion, Myo Devices, and a few other gadgets. We'll have access to several APIs, and a few Datasets will be made available for this event.


Unbelievable speakers, judges and mentors

Kick-starting the hackathon with talks from senior excecutives from leading Health Education companies and corporations. Mentors consist of technology and industry professionals. The judging panel formed by VCs, investors and industry experts. TELHack aims to provide the right environment to ensure all attendees have access to expert advice and judgement throughout the whole event, in order for them to truly build software that will revolutionize Health Education as we know it.
