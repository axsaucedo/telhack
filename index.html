Health Education England's signature hackathon aimed at transforming healthcare education UK-wide.

TELHack is the UK’s largest initiative to innovate the health professions’ education arena and position the UK as the number one country for innovation in healthcare education in the world. TELHack is a Hack Partners event which is being run under the NHS Health Education England (HEE) Technology Enhanced Learning (TEL) programme.

The TELHack 2015 will take place in a unique venue, relevant to the initiative itself ­in London. This initiative will bring together the world’s brightest technologists, global tech giants, the largest UK healthcare education players, and public bodies such as the NHS and HEE on a mission to revolutionise healthcare education.

Innovating healthcare education

TELHack is Health Education England's flagship hackathon aimed at innovating healthcare education. A leading event bringing together80 of the best developers, designers and industry experts from all around Europe to conceive, build and launch high impact solutions that will revolutionise healthcare education.


Cutting edge gadgets, APIs, Datasets

We will be providing a set of gadgets, access to APIs and Datasets relevant to the healthcare education sector that will help participants in their Startup journeys. Throughout the whole weekend, there will be access to 3D printers, Google Glass, Oculus Rift, Leap Motion, Myo Devices, and other kit and equipment. There will be access to several APIs, and selected Datasets will be made available.


Unbelievable speakers, judges and mentors

The hackathon will be kick-started with talks from senior executives from leading healthcare companies and corporations. Mentors will be technology and industry professionals and the e judging panel will include venture capitalists, investors and industry experts. TELHack aims to provide the right environment to ensure all attendees have access to expert advice and judgement throughout the whole event, in order for them to truly build solutions that will revolutionise healthcare education as we know it.


Let's make this happen

This hackathon is organised by Hack Partners - the same organization that ran the StartupBus UK 2015, the HackTrain 2015, StartupHacks, HackaGlobal, and tens of other internationally renowned hackathons - this hackathon will certainly be full of amazing surprises that will amaze all the participants. TELHack is the largest impact UK hackathon aimed at innovating healthcare education and will be full of surprises designed to amaze! An unforgettable experience where 80 of the best developers, designers and entrepreneurs from all around Europe will change the industry for good!
