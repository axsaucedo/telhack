Health Education England's signature hackathon targeted to revolutionise Health Education.

TELHack is the UK’s largest initiative to innovate the health professions’ education arena and position the UK as the number one country for innovation in healthcare education in the world. TELHack is a Hack Partners event which is being run under the NHS Health Education England (HEE) Technology Enhanced Learning (TEL) programme.

The TELHack 2015 will take place in a unique venue, relevant to the initiative itself ­ for example the N​ational Science Museum in London. This initiative will bring together the world’s brightest technologists, the Silicon Valley tech giants, the largest healthcare education players around the UK, and public bodies such as the NHS and HEE on a mission to revolutionise health education.

Who's behind the hackathon?

TELHack is being led by NHS Health Education England. It will be their flagship hackathon aimed to innovate the health industry.

Who will be there?

50 of the best developers, designers and industry experts from all around Europe will be selected by the Hack Partners team.


Where will it be?

Friday 6th November - Opening event - IDEALondon

Saturday 7th & Sunday 8th November - Main event - Interchange Triangle

Tuesday 10th November - Post hackathon event - The Science Museum


What will happen?

Opening event - Participants will hear from industry experts about their business challenges, then there'll be pitching, voting and team formation.

Main event - Come early, get hacking. We'll keep the food and drink coming regularly as well as mentors to help you with your ideas.

Post hackathon event - A final chance to meet industry experts and share your work from the weekend in the incredible setting of the Science Museum.


What tools will be available?

We will be providing a set of gadgets, access to APIs and Datasets relevant to the retail industry that will help participants in their Startup journeys. Throughout the whole weekend, we will provide access to 3D printers, Google Glass, Oculus Rift, Leap Motion, Myo Devices, and a few other gadgets. We'll have access to several APIs, and a few Datasets will be made available for this event.


Will there be anyone else there other than participants?

Kick-starting the hackathon with talks from senior excecutives from leading Health Education companies and corporations. Mentors consist of technology and industry professionals. The judging panel formed by VCs, investors and industry experts. TELHack aims to provide the right environment to ensure all attendees have access to expert advice and judgement throughout the whole event, in order for them to truly build software that will revolutionize Health Education as we know it.


Who's organising the hackathon?

TELHack is being organized by Hack Partners - the same organization that ran the StartupBus UK 2015, the HackTrain 2015, StartupHacks, HackaGlobal. As ever, this hackathon will be full of amazing surprises, don't miss out and apply now.
